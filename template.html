About

After a long night of marking illegible scribble and regretting an academic career path, Dr Surya Singh decided it was about time universities used computers to increase productivity. This revolutionary idea has captivated the hearts and minds of students, educators and the computer-literate everywhere. If this idea gets any better, it will explode.

Numerous copycats have sprung up to compete with Platypus. They will all be crushed in due course. For now, a collection of bugs known as Platypus 1 is giving us all a headache. Platypus 2, which will actually work, is under active development.
